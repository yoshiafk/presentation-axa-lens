Powerful Features

AI-Powered Document Reading

Automatically extracts text from photos of boarding passes and passports with 90% accuracy. Our advanced OCR (Optical Character Recognition) system uses multiple validation layers and intelligent error correction to ensure reliable data extraction. Tested on 150+ real documents from major Indonesian airlines.

Smart Passport Verification

Reads and validates Indonesian passport information using the machine-readable zone (MRZ) at the bottom of passports. Features automatic format validation, multi-language support for Indonesian terms, and built-in error correction. Single-screen verification reduces process from traditional 3+ screens to just 1.

MRZ Reading ICAO Standard
Format Support Indonesian Passport
Verification Steps 1 Screen vs 3+
Data Validation Automatic Checks

Intelligent Route Recognition

Comprehensive database of 100+ Indonesian airports with intelligent route recognition and matching algorithms. Automatically identifies departure and destination airports from boarding passes, even with typos or OCR errors. Supports major domestic routes and international destinations across Southeast Asia, East Asia, and Middle East.
